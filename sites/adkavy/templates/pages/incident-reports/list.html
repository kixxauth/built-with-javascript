Understand The Risks

These incident reports are a tool to help mitigate risk, but in no way are they a complete representation of the avalanche hazards in the backcountry. We encourage users to take avalanche education courses, make their own observations, and understand the consequences of traveling in avalanche terrain, especially in the Adirondacks where there is no forecasting service. Use at your own risk.
