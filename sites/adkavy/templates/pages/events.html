December 2, 2023

Backcountry Ski Kickoff Party!

Walter Breeman Performing Arts Center, North Country School

Join us to kick off the backcountry ski season right!! An evening to inspire and educate the backcountry community to protect and care for winter landscapes. Come on down to get psyched for winter!

What's Happening:

Doors 5:45 pm | Presentation 6:00 - 6:45 pm at North County School

ADK Avy Presentation! Anna Carpenter, Director of Saint Lawrence University Outdoor Program & friend of ADK Avy will present on snow safety, education, & avalanche awareness.

Greetings 7:15 pm | Films 7:30 pm at North Country School Greetings from non-profits ADK Avy, APSA, BETA, & ADK Action!

Films:

  • New Age of Reason
    Featuring Pep Fujas, from WNDR Alpine

  • Revenge of the Topside Boys
    From Nikolai Schirmer & Black Crows

  • The Meaningless Pursuit of Snow
    From Patagonia

Be sure to stick around for raffles & giveaways during intermission!

$10 suggested donation support local backcountry skiing advocacy programs and groups and/or canned goods for the local food pantry.

North Country School
Walter Breeman Performing Arts Center (WallyPAC)
4382 Cascade Rd. Lake Placid, NY 12946
